General Maintenance Technician at The Alabama Institute for the Deaf and Blind

(December 2023-Current)

  • Construction Knowledge: Acquiring hands-on experience in carpentry and general labor tasks.
  • Tool Proficiency: Becoming skilled in the use of various hand and power tools essential to construction work.

  • Physical Stamina: Demonstrating endurance and stamina to handle physically demanding tasks on construction sites.
  • Communication Skills: Effectively communicating with team members to coordinate tasks and ensure smooth project execution.
  • Adaptability: Adjusting to different construction projects and tasks as needed.
  • Time Management: Efficiently organizing and managing time to meet project deadlines.
  • Clean-Up and Maintenance: Performing site clean-up and routine maintenance tasks to ensure a safe and organized work environment.
