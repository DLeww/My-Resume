Wash-It-Off Car Wash Attendant

(February 2023-August 2023)

  • Customer Service: Providing friendly and efficient service to car wash customers.
  • Attention to Detail: Ensuring thorough cleaning of vehicles and attention to small details.

  • Time Management: Completing tasks efficiently in a fast-paced car wash environment.

  • Up-selling: Recommending additional car care services to customers.

  • Cash Handling: Managing transactions and processing payments from customers.
  • Vehicle Inspection: Identifying and reporting any issues with customers' vehicles before and after the wash.
